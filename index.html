Operating principles

01

Do more with less.

Efficiency is a design constraint, not a post-processing step.

02

Design for recovery.

Robust systems anticipate imperfect inputs, degraded states, and real-world pressure.

03

Keep the loop observable.

Every meaningful change needs evidence, a stop condition, and a path to correction.
